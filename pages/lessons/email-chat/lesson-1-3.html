Async vs. Sync Etiquette

In a global MNC, you'll be working across timezones. Mastering asynchronous communication (Email, Slack, Jira) vs. synchronous communication (Calls, Real-time chat) is vital for productivity.

When to use What?

Channel Best for... Expectation
Email Official approvals, long-form logic, documentation. 24-hour response
Slack / Teams Quick updates, casual team syncs, urgent blockers. 1-2 hour response
Meeting / Call Complex debates, conflict resolution, brainstorming. Real-time

The 'Hand-off' Message

When you finish your shift and Hand-off work to a colleague in another timezone, clarity is everything.

Perfect Hand-off: "I've completed the [X] module. The code is pushed to branch [Y]. I've left a few comments on the PR regarding the [Z] logic. Over to you!"
