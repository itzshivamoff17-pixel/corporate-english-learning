Module 1.1

The Modern Business Email

Emails are the permanent record of your professional communication. In an MNC, a clear email saves time and prevents technical debt in communication.

The 'One-Read' Rule

Your goal is for the recipient to understand exactly what you need after reading your email exactly once.

Subject Line Strategy

❌ "Question about the project"

✅ "[Action Required] Approval needed for Payment Gateway API Key - Due EOD"

The 3-Part Structure

  1. The Context: Why are you writing?
  2. The Details: What is the core information? (Use bullet points!)
  3. The Call to Action: What do you need them to do?
