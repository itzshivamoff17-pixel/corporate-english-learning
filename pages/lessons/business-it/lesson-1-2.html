MNC Formalities & Workplace Etiquette

Working in a global MNC requires an understanding of diverse cultural norms and professional levels of formality. What works in a local startup might not work in a Fortune 500 company.

Power Distance & Addressing Colleagues

In most modern tech MNCs, "Sir/Ma'am" is rarely used. However, "politeness" is still paramount. The usage of first names is common, but should be paired with respectful language.

Do

  • Use first names: "Hi Sarah,"
  • Use "Could you please..."
  • Respect timezones

Don't

  • Use "Sir/Madam"
  • Send late-night pings
  • Be overly direct/blunt

The 'Soft' Approach to Feedback

Global teams often use the 'Feedback Sandwich' or 'Softening' techniques when discussing blockers or bugs.

Softening Language

Instead of: "Your API is broken."

Use: "I've noticed some unexpected behavior in the API response. Could we take a look together?"
