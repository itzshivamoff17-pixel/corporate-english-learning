Strategic Networking Phrases

Networking is not about selling—it's about building relationships. For engineers, this often happens at tech meetups, lunch rooms, or virtual 'coffee chats'.

Ice-Breakers for Engineers

At a Tech Conference

"I really enjoyed your point about [Topic]. How are you handling [Specific Challenge] at your organization?"

During a Virtual Coffee Chat

"I've been following your work on [Project]. I'd love to learn more about how your team approaches [Architecture Pattern]."

Closing the Conversation

Always end with an 'Open Door'—a way to stay in touch without being pushy.

Pro Tip: "It was great chatting with you! I'd love to stay in touch—are you on LinkedIn or X (Twitter)?"
