Corporate Vocabulary & Phrases Module 75: Intellectual Property
Legal - Module 75/100

Intellectual Property

Protecting ideas.

Copyright

Meaning: Right to copy.

Example: "The images are under copyright."

Trademark

Meaning: Brand symbol protection.

Example: "We registered the logo as a trademark."

Patent

Meaning: Invention protection.

Example: "We filed a patent for the algorithm."

License

Meaning: Permission to use.

Example: "The software uses an MIT license."

Infringement

Meaning: Breaking IP law.

Example: "We received a copyright infringement notice."

Open source

Meaning: Publicly available code.

Example: "We contribute to open source projects."

Trade secret

Meaning: Confidential business info.

Example: "The recipe is a trade secret."

Royalty

Meaning: Payment for use.

Example: "We pay royalties to the artist."

Domain

Meaning: Website address.

Example: "We bought the .com domain."

Fair use

Meaning: Limited use without permission.

Example: "This falls under fair use."
