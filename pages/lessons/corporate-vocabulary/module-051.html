Remote Work Vocabulary

Terms for valid distributed teams.

Async

Meaning: Asynchronous (not happening at same time).

Example: "We work async because of time zones."

Distributed

Meaning: Spread out.

Example: "We are a fully distributed team."

Hybrid

Meaning: Mix of office and remote.

Example: "We have a hybrid work policy."

Time zone

Meaning: Region with standard time.

Example: "What time zone are you in?"

Overlap

Meaning: Time when both people are working.

Example: "We only have 2 hours of overlap."

Wfh

Meaning: Work From Home.

Example: "I am WFH today."

Digital nomad

Meaning: Person earning a living working online from various locations.

Example: "He is a digital nomad living in Bali."

Sync

Meaning: Synchronize (meet at same time).

Example: "Let's sync up later."

Availability

Meaning: When you are free.

Example: "Please update your availability on the calendar."

Retreat

Meaning: Company gathering.

Example: "The annual company retreat is in Mexico."
