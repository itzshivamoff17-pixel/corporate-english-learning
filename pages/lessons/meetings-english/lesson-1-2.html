Spoken English for Agile Ceremonies

Beyond the daily stand-up, there are three key ceremonies where an engineer's voice is critical: Sprint Planning, Grooming (Refinement), and Retrospectives.

Sprint Planning

Goal: Commit to tasks accurately.

Key Phrasing: "Iâ€™m confident we can deliver this, assuming the CI/CD pipeline remains stable."

Backlog Refinement

Goal: Clarify requirements.

Key Phrasing: "Could we delve deeper into the edge cases for this user story? I'm concerned about the [specific condition]."

Sprint Retrospective

Goal: Continuous improvement.

Key Phrasing: "One thing that worked well was our pair programming session on the legacy module. However, we could improve our documentation frequency."

Pro Tip: Asking for Clarification

Instead of "I don't understand," say "Just so I'm clear, are you saying that [Repeat your understanding]?"
