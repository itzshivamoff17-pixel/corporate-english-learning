Module 1.5

Explaining Technical Concepts

Simplify complex logic for non-technical stakeholders.

The Bridge Technique

Non-technical stakeholders don't need to know "How" it works; they need to know "What it is" and "Why it impacts the business." Use analogies to bridge the gap.

The Analogy Power Play

Instead of: "We’re horizontally scaling the Kubernetes pods to handle the ingress surge..."

Use: "It’s like adding more lanes to a highway during rush hour. It ensures the traffic (data) keeps moving without delays."
