Module 1.4

Reporting Progress

Structuring weekly updates and project status reports for management.

The Executive Summary Approach

Managers and stakeholders care about outcomes, not just activities. Use a "Highlight - Insight - Action" structure for your reports.

The H-I-A Structure

  • Highlight: What happened? "We successfully migrated the production database."
  • Insight: Why does it matter? "This reduces our latency by 15% and saves $2k/month."
  • Action: What's next? "Next week, we are starting the cache optimization phase."
